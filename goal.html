My goal in life

I used to be a person who knew nothing about life, without any goal or destination; I let my parents choose everything for me. But then I realized this is not how you are supposed to live your life.

After learning about people like Steve Jobs, Bill Gates, and Mark Zuckerberg, I know what I want to do. I want to do something for the world, like them. They all contribute to the world in some way. Like Mark Zuckerberg's Facebook, it helps connect people all around the world. I also want to create something that is useful.

Even though I don't know what to do, the goal is clear. I want to help the world in some way.
