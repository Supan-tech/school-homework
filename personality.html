My Personality

People today frequently ignore personality and simply value attractiveness. I think beauty is important, but personality is more important.

I am not perfect, but I always try to be the best version of myself. I'm a very friendly person, but I become very aggressive when I get serious. I become very talkative around close friends. I don't have a lot of friends, as I am not a very social person.

If I were to rate myself, I would give myself a 5/10.
